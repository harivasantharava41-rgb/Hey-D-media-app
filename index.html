✨ What We Offer

App Features

👤

User Authentication

Secure registration and login system with session management and password protection.

📸

Photo & Video Upload

Share your moments by uploading photos and videos directly to your feed.

❤️

Like Posts

Show appreciation for content by liking posts from people you follow.

💬

Comments

Engage with posts by leaving comments and starting conversations.

➕

Follow System

Follow and unfollow users to curate your personalized feed.

📋

User Profiles

View user profiles with all their posts, followers and following count.

📰

Live Feed

Real-time feed showing latest posts from all users in the platform.

🚀

CI/CD Deployment

Automated deployment using Jenkins pipeline with Docker on AWS EC2.
